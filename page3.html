3 camps for your children in 2021

currently we have available 3 camps from which you can choose
Camp Sunshine in Danube Delta

the Danube Delta is the perfect place for learning about Life on Land and Life Below Water, while also learning about endangered species and their importance in our planet's ecosystem. 

Camp Always in Nature in Apuseni Mountains

Apuseni Mountains offer kids an enjoyable experience, while breathing all that good, fresh air and learn about Climate Action.

Camp Child Heart in Lunca Bradului

Lunca Bradului is the perfect place in which kids can learn about team work and communication, while also spending time in nature.
teaching them about Life on Land is a sure thing.

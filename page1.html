about
discover camps

discover camps aims to fulfill
the traditional education system

our project's goal is to take children up to 12 years old through a journey that will complete the formal education that they get in schools.

discover camps develops self understanding, problem solving, team work and proactivity, all through activities and games, coordinated by international volunteers.

the impact that we want is a positive change in Romania!

discover camps is organized
in partnership with AIESEC

AIESEC is a youth-led leadership movement, born in 1948, that has as a "why" peace and fulfillment of humankinds potential.
AIESEC fights for a world in which each and every human being can live peace in their own individual way, accepting, at the same time,
the diversity of the people around them. AIESEC fights for a world in which each human being can reach their full potential and become
the best version of themselves.



quality education

The Sustainable Development Goals, are 17 goals that United Nations (UN)
created in 2017 in order to make the world a better place for  future generations.

AIESEC joined the fight for a better world, having its projects based on 
the Sustainable Development Goals.

discover camps is based on multiple Sustainable Development Goals 
(Climate Action, Life on Land, Life Below Water), but the main one
that we chose to work on relentlessly is Quality Education.

self aware & empowering others

- emotional inteligence development & learning how to make decisions based on critical thinking; empathy & gratitude development;
- personal values, strengths & weaknesses understanding; learning about the importance of leading by example;
- improving communication skills & learning about team work.

solution oriented

- creativity and innovation thinking development;
- failure management; mindset changing from failure being a bad thing to failure being a learning & growing moment; 
- being opened to new opportunities & risks based on past experiences.

world citizen

- current global issues understanding & learning how to communicate opinions about them;
- finding personal solutions for social issues;
- developing an action plan with clear steps that can be implemented in the society through which real social issues can be solved.
location, length and price

discover camps has a variety of locations, partnering with already existing camps for bringing a change in the world.

the project lasts 2 weeks and has different periods of implementation.

discover camps is free of charge, but if you do like our project and you would like to help us, donations are welcomed (contact us for details).

frequently asked questions

who are the international trainers?

the international trainers are carefully selected  young volunteers from all over the globe, that are willing to make a change in the world and to make a change in themselves.

no worries, they go through a few steps before being selected and they also go through a week full of trainings before the starting dates of the camps.

who created the curriculum of discover camps?

the curriculum of discover camps was created in collaboration with teachers, child development specialists and psychologists, all dedicated to creating new methods of teaching kids in a non-formal way, through activities and games.
